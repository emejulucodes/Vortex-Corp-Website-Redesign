Sal Maroni

a
of :
Close X

The man responsible for turning District Attorney Harvey Dent into Two-Face, crime lord Salvatore “Boss” Maroni is infamous in Gotham's underworld.

Sal Maroni is a notorious Gotham City mafia boss with a long track record of funding less-than-reputable businesses, including some with ties to madmen like Hugo Strange. Used to getting exactly what he wants and making a profit in the process, Maroni didn’t react well to being challenged in court and infamously organized the courtroom attack that transformed district attorney Harvey Dent into the scarred, obsessed and murderous psychopath known as Two-Face. Though Maroni has never worn a costume or a mask, time and again he’s proven a perpetual thorn in the Batman’s side, his mob activities carving away at Gotham’s core.
